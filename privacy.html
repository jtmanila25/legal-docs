Pop Fly Scorecard Privacy Policy


Last Updated: [05/09/2025]


Klinger Apps operates the Pop Fly Scorecard mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.


1. Information We Collect


We collect the following types of information:

• Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app.

• Purchase Data: When you subscribe through the App Store, RevenueCat securely processes and tracks your purchase information using your Apple ID.

• Crash Logs and Analytics: We may use third-party services (like RevenueCat, Apple, or others) to collect app performance and crash data.


We do not collect personal identifiers such as name, email, or contact details unless you voluntarily provide them via support.


2. How We Use Your Information


We use the information we collect to:

• Provide and maintain the app

• Process and manage subscriptions

• Improve app performance and user experience

• Respond to support requests


3. Third-Party Services


We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat does not receive any personal information about you—only anonymized purchase data from Apple.


We may also use Apple-provided analytics or crash reporting tools.


4. Data Retention


We retain aggregated and anonymized data for as long as needed to support our analytics and business needs.


5. Your Rights


You can opt out of Apple analytics via your device settings. If you contact us directly, you may request access to or deletion of any information you have voluntarily provided.


6. Children’s Privacy


The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
